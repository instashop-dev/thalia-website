## The Solution
Spreadr became the lifeline of SaumyasStore's catalogue strategy. Using Amazon Product Import, Affiliate Product Linking, Auto Sync, and Inventory Sync together, Sanskriti's team imported over 38,000 products — with automatic removal of listings the moment they go out of stock on Amazon.

### Key Features Used
- **Amazon Product Import** — Import Amazon listings at large scale, forming the foundation of the store's entire product catalogue.
- **Auto Sync & Inventory Sync** — Automatically detect and remove products the moment they go out of stock on Amazon, keeping the storefront accurate.
- **Affiliate Product Linking** — Earn commission on every Amazon product sold through the store via affiliate tracking.
